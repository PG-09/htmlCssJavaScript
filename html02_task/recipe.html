Grandma's Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

flour

suger

butter

eggs

extract

baking soda

mixing (e.g., chocolate chips)

Instructions

   In a bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients.

   In a separate bowl, cream together the butter and sugars until light and fluffy.

   Add the eggs and vanilla extract to the creamed mixture and mix until fully combined.

   Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until no white specks remain.

   Fold in your desired mix-ins, such as chocolate chips or nuts.

   Scoop spoonfuls of dough onto a lined baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.

   Bake for 9-15 minutes, depending on the size of the cookies, until the edges are golden brown.

Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a cooling rack.

Note

Enjoy your freshly baked cookies! Store leftovers in an airtight container to keep them fresh
